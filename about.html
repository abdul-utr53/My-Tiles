About Florenza

2010

Founding Year

Inception: Florenza was founded by a team of flooring enthusiasts with a vision to redefine flooring solutions for homeowners and businesses alike. Their goal was to combine quality materials with exceptional service.What started as a simple idea steadily grew into a clear direction. With every step, we focused on building a foundation of trust, clarity, and purpose. That vision continues to guide us today.

2013

Initial Launch

Business Setup: Florenza established its headquarters in New York, partnering with top manufacturers to offer a diverse range of flooring products, including hardwood, laminate, vinyl, and tile.Our first launch introduced a carefully crafted collection designed to reflect who we are and what we stand for. Every product was chosen with intention, balancing style, durability, and everyday usability. The response strengthened our belief in meaningful design. It marked the moment our story truly came alive.

2010-2026

Our History

Know us: Florenza was established with a strong belief in transforming ordinary floors into extraordinary foundations of beauty and strength. From humble beginnings as a small flooring service, we have grown into a trusted brand recognized for quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction.Our journey is defined by continuous innovation, dedication to craftsmanship, and a passion for excellence. Each project we complete reflects our commitment to durability and elegance.Through decades of experience, Florenza has mastered the art of blending traditional techniques with modern design trends. Today, our name stands as a symbol of trust and perfection in the flooring industry. We continue to evolve, innovate, and serve our customers with pride and integrity.

Always

Our Mission

Your Believe: Our mission at Florenza is to deliver premium-quality flooring solutions that combine durability, beauty, and sustainability. We aim to enhance every space by providing flooring that meets both functional and aesthetic needs. Our team is committed to excellence in every stage — from consultation and design to installation and after-service support. We continuously adopt the latest technologies and trends to ensure our products remain at the forefront of innovation. Our focus is on building long-term relationships with customers through honesty, reliability, and superior craftsmanship. We believe every floor tells a story, and our mission is to make that story timeless. By maintaining strict quality standards and using eco-friendly materials, we strive to create spaces that are both stylish and sustainable. Our goal is not just to meet customer expectations but to consistently exceed them. Florenza stands for precision, passion, and perfection in every project we undertake.

2025-Onwards

Our Vision

Our Future: Our vision is to become a global leader in the flooring and surface design industry, known for our innovation, integrity, and excellence. We aspire to redefine the flooring experience by merging technology, creativity, and sustainability. Florenza envisions a future where every home and workspace reflects comfort, class, and individuality through thoughtfully designed floors. We aim to inspire people by offering flooring that not only looks stunning but also lasts for generations. Our vision includes empowering our team with skills and tools to deliver cutting-edge solutions that shape modern living. We seek to expand our presence worldwide, bringing our craftsmanship to new markets and communities. By focusing on eco-conscious production and responsible business practices, we aim to build a better, greener tomorrow. Every design choice we make reflects our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Ultimately, our vision is to make Florenza a name synonymous with flooring excellence, creativity, and trust.

Florenza — your one-stop destination for premium tiles and flooring solutions that bring elegance, durability, and style to every space.
