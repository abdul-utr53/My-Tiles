floor refinishing

Floor refinishing for a company like Florenza focuses on restoring the original beauty and durability of existing floors. The process includes sanding, buffing, and applying new finishes or coatings to remove scratches, stains, and dullness. Whether it’s hardwood, marble, or tile, Florenza ensures each surface regains its shine and strength, giving your space a refreshed and elegant appearance.

floor repair

Floor repair by Florenza involves identifying and fixing issues such as cracks, chips, uneven surfaces, or water damage. Skilled technicians assess the problem and use advanced tools and materials to restore the floor’s stability and look. From tile replacements to structural fixes, Florenza ensures long-lasting solutions that blend seamlessly with the existing design.

Explore Designs That Inspire

Ceramic tiles are durable, affordable, and perfect for everyday use. They come in many designs and are ideal for walls and floors.

Porcelain tiles are stronger and water-resistant, making them great for high-traffic areas. Their premium finish gives spaces a modern look.

Marble tiles add luxury with their natural veining and glossy finish. They instantly elevate living rooms, halls, and premium interiors.

Granite tiles are extremely tough and scratch-resistant. They are perfect for kitchens, outdoor areas, and heavy-use spaces.

Mosaic tiles bring vibrant patterns and creativity to walls. They work beautifully in bathrooms, kitchens, and decorative sections.

Subway tiles offer a classic, clean look for modern interiors. They are commonly used for kitchen backsplashes and bathroom walls.

Vitrified tiles have low porosity and high strength, making them long-lasting. Their sleek finish is great for homes and offices.

Terracotta tiles bring a warm, earthy feel to spaces. Their rustic look is ideal for patios, balconies, and traditional interiors.
