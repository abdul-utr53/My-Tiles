Frequently Asked Question

1. What products does Florenza offer?

Florenza offers a wide range of high-quality tiles, flooring materials, and decorative surfaces for both residential and commercial spaces. Our collection includes ceramic tiles, porcelain tiles, marble, wooden flooring, and more.

2. Can I order custom designs or sizes?

Yes, Florenza provides customization options depending on the product type and availability. You can contact our design team to discuss your specific requirements and get a personalized solution.

3. Do you provide installation services?

Absolutely. Florenza offers professional installation services through our trained experts to ensure perfect fitting and long-lasting results for all flooring and tiling projects.

4. How can I get a quote for my project?

You can request a free quote by filling out the contact form on our website or visiting our nearest showroom. Our team will review your requirements and share a detailed estimate promptly.

5. Are Florenza products suitable for outdoor use?

Yes, we have a dedicated range of tiles and flooring materials designed specifically for outdoor spaces such as patios, driveways, and terraces, offering both durability and weather resistance.

6. How do I maintain and clean Florenza tiles or floors?

Our tiles and flooring materials are easy to maintain. Regular sweeping and mopping with mild cleaners are enough to keep them shining. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage the surface finish.

7. Where can I see Florenza’s latest designs?

You can explore our latest collections on the official Florenza website under the “Products” section or visit our social media pages for inspiration, design ideas, and new arrivals.

Florenza — your one-stop destination for premium tiles and flooring solutions that bring elegance, durability, and style to every space.
